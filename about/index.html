About Agentix

Strategic advisory with certified Zoom delivery and support expertise.

Agentix helps enterprises design, deploy, and optimize AI-powered customer engagement and communications. We are a Strategic Advisor Partner with Zoom and certified for deployment and support across Zoom Phone, Zoom Contact Center, and Zoom Virtual Agent solutions.

Leadership

Technical CX leaders who know the space - and ship the work.

Agentix leadership brings together CX, AI, and service operations practitioners with deep technical fluency and hands-on delivery experience. Founded in California by Edward Moon and Berkeley Vogelheim, and joined by Michael Stewart, Agentix helps organizations move from strategy to implementation with clarity, speed, and discipline.
