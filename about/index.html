About Agentix

A technology partner with certified Zoom deployment and support expertise.

Agentix helps enterprises design, deploy, and optimize AI-powered customer engagement and communications. We are a Gold Partner with Zoom and certified for deployment and support across Zoom Phone, Zoom Contact Center, and Zoom AI Virtual Agents solutions.

Leadership

Technical CX leaders who know the space and ship the work.

Agentix brings together a team of CX, AI, and engineering talent with deep technical fluency and hands-on delivery experience. Founded in California by Eddie Moon and Berk Vogelheim, and joined by Michael Stewart, Agentix helps organizations move from strategy to implementation with clarity, speed, and discipline.

Eddie Moon

Principal

Eddie brings over 17 years of technical and business experience across Big 4 consulting firms, Fortune 100 corporations, pre-IPO organizations, and early-stage startups. He served as a Principal Solution Engineer at Genesys and was an early CX Specialist at Zoom. His background includes management consulting at Deloitte and KPMG, combined with sales engineering and product specialist roles across AI, CCaaS, UCaaS, CRM, and ITSM cloud technologies. His certifications include Genesys Cloud Developer, AWS Cloud Practitioner, ITIL 4, CompTIA ITF+, Demo2Win, and Zoom ZSA, ZTSA, and ZDA.
